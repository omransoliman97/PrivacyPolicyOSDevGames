# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: May 22, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application, **Premium Dice Roller 3D** (the "App").

---

## 1. Information Collection and Use

### Personal Data
We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, or physical address.

### Non-Personal and Usage Data
We may collect non-personal information automatically through third-party services integrated within the App. This is used solely to provide and improve the App's functionalities.
- **Preferences and App Settings:** We store your preferences locally on your device (such as the dice display mode and total sum toggle) using standard local storage mechanisms.
- **Rolling Credits:** Your remaining roll credits are stored locally on your device.

---

## 2. Third-Party Services and Advertisements

To support the development and provide the App for free, we display advertisements using **Google AdMob**. AdMob may collect and use information about your device, including:
- Device identifiers (such as Android Advertising ID).
- Network and connectivity information.
- Approximate location (IP-based).
- Ad interaction data.

For more information on how Google collects and uses your data, please review the [Google Partner Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites) and [Google AdMob Privacy Policy](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543).

---

## 3. Children's Privacy

The App is designed for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected any personal information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to delete such information.

---

## 4. Security

We value your trust in providing us your device data, thus we strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. But remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

---

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
