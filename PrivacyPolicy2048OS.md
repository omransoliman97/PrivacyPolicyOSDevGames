# Privacy Policy

**2048 OS**  
Last updated: March 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to 2048 OS. We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about how our app works. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Data You Provide
2048 OS does not require account registration. No name, email address, or personal identification is collected directly by us.

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Data

**Analytics (Firebase Analytics)**  
We use Firebase Analytics to understand how players interact with the app. This may include:
- App usage events (e.g. game started, difficulty selected)
- Device type and operating system version
- Country/region (aggregated)
- Session duration

This data is anonymised and used solely to improve the game experience.

**Crash Reporting (Firebase Crashlytics)**  
If the app crashes, Crashlytics automatically collects crash logs to help us diagnose and fix issues. This may include device model, OS version, and the state of the app at the time of the crash. No personally identifiable information is included.

**Advertising (Google AdMob)**  
2048 OS displays ads through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalised or non-personalised ads, including:
- Device identifiers (e.g. advertising ID)
- IP address
- Approximate location
- Interaction with ads

For more information on how Google uses this data, please visit:  
https://policies.google.com/privacy

---

## 3. Rewarded Ads

2048 OS offers optional rewarded ads that allow you to earn in-game benefits (hints or hearts). Watching these ads is entirely voluntary. The reward granted is determined solely by the AdMob reward configuration — no additional reward is given by the app beyond what AdMob specifies.

---

## 4. Data Storage

Game progress (your current puzzle, stats, and settings) is stored **locally on your device** using on-device storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

---

## 5. Children's Privacy

2048 OS is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data through the app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

## 7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your data, including:
- The right to access information collected about you
- The right to request deletion of your data
- The right to opt out of personalised advertising

To opt out of personalised ads on your device:
- **Android**: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
- **iOS**: Settings → Privacy → Tracking → disable for 2048 OS
